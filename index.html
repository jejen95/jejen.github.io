About Me

jejen is a Bogor-born photographer and videographer. , jejen has always had love for nature - it is what inspires him every day to create and to use the combination of people & nature to tell compelling visual stories. He speaks indonesia. jejen developed a passion for photography in high school and fell in love with the creative process. After graduating, Jejen has dedicated his time to building a diverse portfolio, and has successfully accumulated a vast social media presence ever since. Jejen hopes to be able to inspire others and to let his audience experience the through his lens.
